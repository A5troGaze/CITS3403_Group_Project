How to Play

A bug is running across your screen. Your job: keep it alive for 500 m by physically adjusting your monitor's brightness in response to incoming obstacles.

The three obstacles
  • Flash — the screen fills near-white. Tap the on-screen Dim button 3 times.
  • Blackout — the screen fills near-black. Tap the on-screen Brighten button 3 times.
  • Flicker — rapid white/black alternation (late game only). Smash the B key three times fast to survive.
Scoring

Score is total time survived in seconds (lower is better, like a speedrun). Each fast confirm shaves 1 s off your time. Reach 500 m to win.
