Frequently Asked Questions

(for the people who are dumb and can't figure stuff out themselves)

How do you play the game?

The process is simple. Simply, go through the different tasks with patience and do them quickly, the timer's running.

How do I sign in / register?

Simply go to the sign in / register tab in the header and fill out your details.

What if I can't complete the game?

There would be an option on the end of the screen allowing you to save your progress or exit completely.

How do I change to dark mode?

Go to the settings page and there should be options to switch between light / dark / follow system settings.
